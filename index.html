How this works & honest limits

A GitHub Action fetches every feed on a schedule and saves the results to a data file. This page just reads that file, so it loads instantly.

Two tracks. Practice is reference material you'll actually read — EB, OD, talent, research, plus cultural critique. Culture is stimulus you'll skim for a spark — design, film, music, gaming, and the rest, filtered toward the branding-adjacent angle.
